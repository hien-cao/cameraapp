About Us

We are Antonio Mesa, Hien Cao, Irene Manzanares, a project team from the course Design in Engineering (ELEC-C9800). In this design project, we are making a system that surveil the printing process of a 3D printer in Sähköpaja - a workshop in Aalto School of Electrical Engineering.

With the system, students can monitor their printing 24/7. Additionally, the system can making an alert with a displayed message so that people in the workshop are able to help stop the printer.

We would like to thank you Salu Ylirisku, Pyry Vikberg and other assistants in the Sähköpaja for their instructions and supports.

Lecturer: Salu Ylirisku

Project instructors: Pyry Vikberg and Tanweer Muhammad
